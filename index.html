Duck farming, in particular, supports water conservation, generates employment for rural communities, and thrives in diverse environments where traditional crops cannot grow. Ducks are resilient and adaptable, making them a profitable option for commercial farming. By integrating innovative techniques with traditional methods, duck farming contributes to agriculture, trade, and industry, offering unique opportunities for growth and sustainability.
